About the Fund

We started out tutoring in our own college, trying to help students understand lessons no matter their circumstances. We created a website to reach out to other people and schools, sharing our resources with schools and students. After many years of growth, we eventually started WSF as a non-profit, bringing education worldwide.


As we started to expand, we realized that in order to help everyone, there needs to be a space for learning online. That is what our secondary mission is. With our wide range of courses in STEM, the humanities, as well as basic life skills, anyone can learn anything, anywhere.

Our Goals

The fund strives to provide free quality education to all students, no matter the circumstances. That is why our new Research and Development division is currently doing studies on how to best enhance a student's learning experience.

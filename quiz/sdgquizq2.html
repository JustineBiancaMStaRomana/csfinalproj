2. How many more teachers are needed to reach the goal of universal primary and secondary education by 2030?

  • 69 million
  • 29 million
  • 49 million
  • 12 million

While the global teacher workforce has increased from 62 million to 94 million teachers between 2000 and 2019, to reach universal primary and secondary education in 2030, 69 million more teachers are needed with 24 million for primary and 44 million for secondary education.
